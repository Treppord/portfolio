About Me

I embarked on my coding journey at the age of 13,
diving into the world of programming through JAVA to create mods for Minecraft.
Over the past 6 years, my passion for coding has flourished,
leading me to explore not only game development but also web technologies.

Gradually, I expanded my skill set to include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript,
broadening my horizons in both programming and design.
My journey has been marked by successful Minecraft mods showcased on platforms like Curseforge.

Currently, I am pursuing my education in Amsterdam at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam.
I am enrolled in the Frontend Design and Development program,
where I continue to refine my skills and knowledge in the dynamic field of web development.
